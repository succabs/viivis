DJ Viivakoodi / DJ-Koodi / Viivis is known for sets that confuse, delight, and sometimes concern. He reads the room and ignores it. A total threat to the dancefloor.

This man does everything: He can play any genre. He mixes effortlessly metal into pop, and rap into psytrance. He can also host karaoke. With him, everything is possible. He is always chasing the dragon.

Dj Viivakoodi is based in Jyväskylä, Finland.
